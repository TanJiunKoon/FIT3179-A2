Which states in Malaysia have the highest poverty rates?

This bump chart visualizes the ranking of states in Malaysia based on their poverty rates over time.

The data shows the absolute poverty rate, which is defined as the proportion of households with a monthly income below the Poverty Line.

Income Growth by State in Malaysia

These visualizations track the total income across various Malaysian states over time, with a detailed breakdown of key regions.

The bottom line chart provides brushing interaction, allowing users to explore specific time periods by highlighting them in the upper graph.

The Total Income represents the sum of all household incomes across various states in Malaysia, with each colored layer representing a state's contribution.

The Average Income is the gross monthly household income averaged across households in each state.
